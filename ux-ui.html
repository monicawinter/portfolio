I created a patterned illustration to make a graphic style octopus t-shirt. The octopus is made up of varying sizes of lines and different patterns.

The t-shirt was designed to have the illustration wrap around from the front to the back of the shirt seamlessly.

The octopus t-shirt design uses dramatic colours to bring the octopus design forward, emphasizing the varying patterns of the piece.
